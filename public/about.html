About Us

Qorald Hub Tools is a tool designed to help users explore and analyze blockchain and cryptocurrency projects hosted on GitHub. Our goal is to provide clear and easy access to key information such as categories, popularity metrics, and links to prominent developer profiles.

Main Features

  • Automatic project categorization: Wallets, DeFi, and more.
  • Repository exploration with relevant information: stars, forks, and owners.
  • Direct access to developer profiles on GitHub.
  • Custom search to find specific projects.

Team

We are a team passionate about blockchain technology and software development. Our mission is to make the open-source community more accessible to developers and investors interested in the cryptocurrency ecosystem.
